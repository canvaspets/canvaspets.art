Custom Hand Painted Pet Portraits

My name is Eric Johnson and my goal is to help create unique memories of your pets through custom hand painted art pieces that can be displayed and enjoyed for a lifetime.

Since I was young, I have always been passionate about art, animals, and computers. My love for two of these led me down a career path into web and software development, but I missed the physical nature of putting lines and color on paper. During COVID, I purchased some canvases and acrylics and began painting our household pets and quickly revived my love and passion for traditional art.

In a world filled with digital snapshots, I believe there's an unparalleled beauty in preserving the spirit of your pets in a tangible and enduring form. Each painting I create is a heartfelt endeavor to immortalize the individuality of your companions – whether it's the twinkle in their eyes, the tilt of their head, or the wag of their tail, I strive to encapsulate those moments that make your pets irreplaceable members of your family.

If you're searching for a unique and personalized pet portrait that goes beyond a simple likeness, you've come to the right place. Let's collaborate to create a masterpiece that celebrates the heart and soul of your beloved pets.

"There is something very special about a pet in the family that only pet owners truly understand. Our pets are our family. They live with us and share our family experiences. The downside is that our pets lives are too short. To compensate for their short lives, our photographs reinforce our memories of them. Eric painted a perfect portrait of our beloved Cooper that 100% captured his heart and spirit. I was emotionally moved when I saw the portrait. I love and cherish it."
